Upload an image! If the image is already Black and White Posterized, that'll be detected automatically. Also you can click on "Upload File" at any point to restart the process, but might be buggy. If you run into any issues/bugs, feel free to just refresh the page and try again. The main work flow should be fine, but if you go back and forth between steps you might uncover a bug. IMPORTANT NOTE: If the image you upload is high resolution (like >1k by 1k pixels) the generation might take some time, just be patient.
